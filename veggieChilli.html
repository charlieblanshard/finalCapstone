Vegetarian Chilli

This is a super versitile dish, good for all year round and it freezes well. It can be served with rice to make the dish more hearty, a wrap to make it more fresh or nachos for a great party dish.

Ingredients
Serves 4
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 bell pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 tins kidney beans
  • 2 fresh chillis
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cube of dark chocolate
  • lime
  • Sour cream and cheese to serve
Instructions

Dice onion, chop pepper into small cubes, mince the garlic and finely chop chillis. Heat some oil in a large frying pan and add the onion and pepper. Fry for about 5 minutes until soft and onion is translucent.

Add the garlic, paprika, cumin, chilli powder and chillis and fry for another 5 minutes.

Add the beans with their water and the tomatoes to the pan. Stir thoroughly to ensure all veg is mixed in to the sauce. Add the chocolate. Let the chilli simmer for 20-25 mins stirring occasionally. If the sauce starts to get too thick add a bit of water.

Serve with desired carb and top with soured cream and cheese.
