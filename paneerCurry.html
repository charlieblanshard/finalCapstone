Paneer, Spinach and Pea Curry

Fairly easy but super impressive dish to cook for a large number or guests, or make a batch and freeze the leftovers.

Ingredients
Serves 4
  • 240g Basmati Rice or naan breads
  • 200g of Paneer
  • 200g of Spinach
  • 200g Peas
  • 2 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 white onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • Spices
  • 2 tsp Turmeric
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tsp dried Coriander
  • 2 tsp Chilli powder-mild or hot
Instructions

Mix the spices on a plate. Cut the paneer into cubes and coat in the spices. Heat oil in the pan and add the paneer, turn occasionally until the sides are brown. Remove from the pan and leave on kitchen roll to drain the oil.

Boil the kettle and

Chop the onion and garlic. If you have a blender, blend the onion, garlic and ginger. Add the frying pan with any remaining spices and fry until soft.

Stir in the chopped tomatoes and 200ml of water. Bring to boil and leave to simmer.

Add the frozen peas and let the sauce reboil.

Add the paneer back to the pan with the spinach and cook until the spinach wilts.

Serve with the rice and enjoy.
