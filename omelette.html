Cheesy Mushroom Omelette

Super easy and cheap but satisfying dish. The mushrooms can be swapped with any veg here. Don't go too overboard on the veg as it'll be hard for the omelette to form. This can be served as a main dish for any meal or as pictured here with some tomato toast with avocado.

Ingredients
Serves 1
  • 2 eggs
  • Handful of grated cheese- any hard cheese works well
  • 200g mushrooms. This can easily be subbed out for another veg.
Instructions

Whisk eggs.

Heat a frying pan, this works better with a smaller pan so when the egg is added it coats the whole pan.

Fry mushrooms on a low heat to enable the water to sweat out. Add a pinch of salt once the liquid has come out. Remove the mushrooms from the pan.

Add the eggs to the pan and swirl around so that the whole pan is coated. Add the mushrooms on top.

Cover the pan, if you do not have a lid you can use a plate.
