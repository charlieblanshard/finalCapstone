Gnocchi Primervia Bake

Super green gnocchi dish, feels healthy with all the veg but is super satisfying with the mozzerella top. If you are tight for time you could serve this all mixed together without baking with some grated cheddar or parmesan on the top.

Ingredients
Serves 4
  • 1 pack Gnocchi
  • 4 cups of any 2 green veg- e.g. courgette, broccoli, beans, peas, leeks
  • 4 handfuls of Spinach
  • 3 tablespoons Pesto
  • Fresh Mozzerella- used reduced fat
Instructions

Preheat the oven to 200¬∞C. Boil the kettle and pour water over gnocchi and leave for 2 mins to soften. Once the 2 minutes are up, drain and put aside.

Cook green veg. If using: courgette or leeks fry until soft. Broccoli, beans, peas can be steamed or boiled until tender.

Add the veg a pan and on a low heat mix in the pesto and spinach, stir until the spinach begins to wilt. Remove from the heat and carefully mix in the gnocchi ensuring it is coated in the pesto.

Pour the gnocchi and veg mix into an ovenproof dish. Tear the mozzerella and scatter on top. Bake in the oven for 20-25mins until the mozzerella has gone slightly brown. Enjoy!
