About Me

Hi my name is Charlie.

I am 24 years old and live in London. I love food both making it and eating out and want to share these experiences with you. I am a pescetarian but mainly make vegetarian food.

Encouraged by my mum at an early age to experiment, cooking whatever and whenever made me realise how adding in a different ingredient could totally change the taste my interest began then. Throughout university I got great pleasure cooking for friends.

Excessive time during lockdown allowed me to experiment further, I found cooking relaxing and it allowed me to be creative. I would look forward to sharing my creations with my family. Mealtimes became a focus, it brought our family together even though we were crammed in the house. You can't beat socialising over a nice meal.

Now life in London gives me access to an array of ingredient. l love hosting dinner parties or knocking up a quick supper.
